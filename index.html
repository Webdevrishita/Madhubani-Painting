Madhubani painting

Welcome to the India's most famous Art Form.
Madhubani art is a style of Indian painting. It is done with a variety of tools, including fingers, twigs, brushes, nib-pens, and matchsticks and using natural dyes and pigments. It is made from the paste of powdered rice. It has five distinctive styles: Bharni, Kachni, Tantrik, Godna and Kohbar. It has played a key role in environmental conservation efforts in India in 2012.
